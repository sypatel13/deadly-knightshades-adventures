# Disappointing the Demon Expert

Outside of [[Blingdenstone]] we met the “slime expert” who questioned us about our travels and encounters with demon lords. However, something seemed off about him, as we noticed certain features weren’t exactly how we remembered him. From our scrying in [[Gauntlgrym]], we also knew that [[Gracklestoog]] had been razed to the ground. Before we became hostile, he revealed himself to be a demon expert named [[Vizian Daveer]].
He was very excited to hear our encounters with [[Orcus]], [[Graz'zt]], [[Demogorgon]], [[Zuggtmgoy]], and [[Juiblex]].

- [[Vizian Daveer]]’s backstory  
  - He was a former demon researcher for the Drow / Menzoberanzan government  
  - He was kicked out after he refused to do cerdian experiments for fear of, oh say, releasing the demon lords onto the underdark  
  - He was replaced by Dimar Ilalorgh, who continued [[Vizian Daveer]]’s work and eventually released the demon lords into the underdark

- Worm writhings / purple worms   
  - Purple worms have a curious qualities regarding time  
  - Time gets all fuckey around them, they naturally are able to rattle the space time continuum   
    - Their eggs are particularly potent  
  - These worms are *very* dangerous and not a lot is known about them (researchers get got)  
* Language Of The Gods   
  * The “language” one can use to speak the True Name ™ of a Demon / Celestial  
  * If you know the True Name of a demon, you have a new insight into their power, if you play your cards right, you can even control them for a short time

  * What [[Quackity]] Saw  
    * (when [[Quackity]] was wackity by Zuggtmoy and was on the back of the giant bird that said “not yet” then woke up with the symbols burnt into his eyes).   
    * That was an imprint of the name of Ra  
    * A totally dumbed down version of his True Name  
  * The magic of the demon will also have an imprint of their name   
    * E.g. something the pour their heart and soul into

* Demon summoning ritual and The Plan ™   
  * The only forces strong enough to destroy the demon lords are the demon lords themselves  
  * If we play our cards right, we can perform a ritual that will rip all the demon lords out of time and space and pull them down to the same place   
    * Also using their True Names ™ we will be able to control them for oh say 12 \- 36 seconds ish give or take
