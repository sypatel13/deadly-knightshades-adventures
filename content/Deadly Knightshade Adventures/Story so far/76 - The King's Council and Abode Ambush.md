# The King’s Council and Abode Ambush

The party talked to [[Zelron Roaringhorn]], a powerful enchanter. After beating the spider web game, he showed the animated suit of armor he promised to us, [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] foaming at the mouth. He explained that it follows the commands of whoever wears its necklace. The only other tidbit was that the armor can store one spell in it at a time, but he forgot what he stored in it last so might wanna stay back.  

[[Kalelgoni|Sprit Guardian Armor, [[Kalelgoni]]’s Vessel]]

We talked to Lanniver Strayl about how he worshiped Tyr.  
A mysterious woman in a hood with a small tattoo under her lip appeared. She pulled [[Quackity]] aside to “investigate a plant.” She acknowledged that she was the one that caught [[Kadulus]] and [[Quackity]] in the brothel. She told [[Quackity]] of an agent by the name of Willow that she had lost contact with in Menzoberranzan and gave him a *slingshot* which he could use to shoot a message/sound across distances (like a ventriloquist).  
After enough time had passed, King Bartemus was getting impatient. He pounded the table and said “Everyone should be here by now\!” We utilized a crystal ball we had to scry on the cities that were supposed to be present. [[Gracklestoog]] was in flames, [[Blingdenstone]] looked barren, and the stone giants were migrating somewhere in rock tunnels.  
The party returned from the uneventful king’s palace to their humble abode when they saw who else other than [[Ilvara]] and her group of darrow had infiltrated their home. Though they could have just walked back outside and called for the town guard, we seized the opportunity to destroy the ones that had brought us to The Underdark in the first place. We were without our weapons due to the king’s meeting, so we each made a mad dash to our respective rooms/beds to retrieve them. Gildo’s *Haste* allowed him to get his fairly quickly, [[Quackity]] dashed for his hammock in the middle of the room, getting knocked unconscious by a gas in the process, and [[Kadulus]] nearly died (like dead dead) trying to get his staff from his room. Cloudkill almost took out the entire party for the gazillionth time in the campaign. Halfway through the battle, Sir Lannyver came bursting through one of the windows and immediately rushed into battle.[[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]]did both damage and heals, Luna almost died with 1HP and a dream, and [[Kalelgoni]] was picking daisies outside. In the end, we slayed [[Ilvara]] and her pathetic army with few casualties of our own.
