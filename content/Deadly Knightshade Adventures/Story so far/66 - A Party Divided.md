# A Party Divided

## Gildo's Bazaar Adventure

Me started out by hyping up [[Luna]] for our exciting trade deal. This will be the biggest trade we have witnessed on our adventures so far. [[Kazuk Pickshine]] mentioned that he wanted to see us before we left, so we made our way to his tent. There he had two things to address: the Khalua's Pass and arranging the trade with [[Errd Blackskull|Commander Blackskull]]. He told me of some superstition the men may have about Kahlua's Pass but not to worry, besides there was no other route that could get us to Gracklesstoog in such a short time. He gave me a map, which seemed to have many routes previously not known to me. [[Kazuk Pickshine]] said to keep it safe, and I swore on [[Waukeen]] that nothing would happen to it, tucking it into my shiny armor. We went into his tent and called [[Errd Blackskull|Blackskull]]. She was in the middle of talking to some short fellow about whether things were under control or not. Just then, some big fire in the streets coming from the ground could be seen. [[Kazuk Pickshine]] finally chimed in and asked for her attention. He brought up the deal and she agreed but was noticeably shooketh. When we started asking questions, she said she was busy and hung up. We made our way to the gate of [[Blingdenstone]], slapping [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]]'s ass in the gauntlet on the way out. There we met a crew of 8 hardy gnomes, all named [[Salt McGee]] \#1-8. [[Salt McGee]] informed me of our shipment of spell gems with salt on top to deter bandits. He also brought up Khalua's Pass but agreed that there was no other way to complete our mission given the time crunch. We headed out after talking out the logistics. [[Luna]] and I had a great talk on the way. There was a lot of downtime so I took the opportunity to ask her how she's been. She acknowledged that I'm far superior than the rest of the party don't tell the others hehe. But then we had quite a nice chat about why I go about my business the way I do. There was a lot of Filosofy, but in the end it seemed like we understood each other better for it. About 1.5 days into our caravan, we crossed Khalua's Pass. It was a daunting stone bridge with tiles missing, hanging over a trench of lava surrounded by stone statues on either side. It gave me the creeps but nothing happened. We eventually made it to [[Gracklestoog]] where we were met by [[Errd Blackskull|Blackskull]]. She seemed...off. She immediately looked at [[Luna]] (polymorphed to look like [[Edith]]) and asked who she was, raising her eyebrows like a horny teenager. I declared that she was simply my associate and nothing more. [[Errd Blackskull|Blackskull]] took this opportunity to call her over and basically asked her out on a date. I was speechless, and [[Errd Blackskull|Blackskull]] seemed a little offended that I wasn't on board. She said our shipment was ready then stormed back to her office. The slime expert also came out when he overheard that there was going to be a slime war. I told him it might be the only chance he got to see that many slimes but I didn't say it good enough so he decided to stay. As we said goodbye, a bunch of flames rose from the ground. I went back over to [[Errd Blackskull|Blackskull]]'s office. Her chair was turned around and she said "hello" then turned around and switched to "Oh, it's you. What do you want?" I tried to ask what was going on in the city but she just seemed super depressed and talked about how she had lost her [[Iliana]]. I eventually gave up and tried to talk to a few other people to no avail. On the way back to [[Blingdenstone]], the bridge on Khalua’s Pass suddenly started to rumble. A bunch of fire imps and goblins spawned flying in the air on the sides of the bridge and a big scary man with chainz arose at the other end of the bridge. The flying pests were taken care of rather easily but the chain man was a hassle. He almost knocked me down and killed two of the veterans. Nonetheless I was able to slay him and we carried on our route. I told [[Luna]] about my sister the rest of the way.

## Quackity’s (Re)Visit to [[Neverlight Grove]]

Not taking any men to slow him down, [[Quackity]] made his way to [[Neverlight Grove]]. The trip would take him about a day and a half. During his travels, he was hiding in a cave when he heard some human voices coming his way, identifying them as paladins and clerics. He decided to disguise himself as [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] and approached the group, talking like Lester. However, the group saw right through his disguise and asked who he was. The seemingly leader, [[Sir Lanniver Strayl]], explained they were a group that had felt the disturbance of demonic presence in the Underdark all the way from the surface and were exploring the source. [[Quackity]] told them of our adventures and pointed on their maps where there had been demons so far. They thanked him and were on their way.  
Quackity eventually made it to [[Neverlight Grove]], although he could tell that nothing was as we left it. The place was barren. He managed to find [[Basidia]] hiding underground. He asked what had happened and she explained that [[Zuggtmgoy]] had taken over [[Neverlight Grove]] and that she was the only one left. She gave [[Quackity]] the butt medicine she had made with the mushrooms we had handed over the last time we saw her. [[Quackity]] explained what ingredients he was looking for and Basidia pointed out where he could find them. He took off in search of the ingredients, telling Basidia he’d be back for her.  
On the way to the ingredients, [[Quackity]] encountered some zombified michonyds. He was able to make it to the grand palace and after a myriad of dex checks to traverse the slimy muddy perimeter, he got the ingredients he needed. When trying to go back, he failed a save and slid down the slope; slamming into the palace. As the alarm was raised, he saw a 20 foot tall figure round the corner. This was good ole [[Zuggtmgoy]], who quickly did a one two tendril wack and pretty much knocked out [[Quackity]]. He had a vision he was on the back of a great bird who told him “Not Yet”, telling him to spread his wings. [[Quackity]] obliged, slowly lifting until he was flying into a bright light. He awoke being dragged to a digestion pod by two zombies. He had the mysterious symbol burnt into his eyes. He was able to do some dope [[Quackity]] moves and escaped the thugs. He retrieved the supplies and succeeded in sneaking Basidia out, gaining the party a massive edge.

## Tholraak's Summoning Stones 

On the way to the summoning circle, saw [[Shushar The Awoken]] and Co**. on the way but flew away. At The Summoning Circle, there were 6 gems on pedestals around the room and 4 lizardfolk, [[Tholraak]] started talking to them and one didn't seem like he actually understood. Turned out to be an impostor. [[Tholraak]] was able to convince the real lizardfolk to attack the impostor. The impostor started summoning shit with the circle, causing a giant goat, a shark, and a T-Rex to be summoned, wreaking havoc. [[Tholraak]] overpowered him but, resorting to summoning a rock elemental, [[Pebbles]], losing 2 lizardfolk and both veterans in the process. On the way back, [[Tholraak]] ran into [[Shushar The Awoken]] again. This time he couldn't be avoided and [[Shushar The Awoken]] claimed to be part of the *Society of Brilliance*, spreading wisdom wherever they went. They shared with [[Tholraak]] that they do not believe [[Kalelgoni]]'s mortal form can be saved. Upon returning to BlingBlingStone, [[Garra Songstone|Garra]] said that [[Pebbles]] is a good boi, and the lizardfolk followed [[Tholraak]]'s orders. He was aggressive but calmed down after orders from [[Tholraak]]. [[Tholraak]] kept the death slaad (gray dood) alive, claiming he would set it free if it answered his questions. He interrogated it, asking where it came from. It claimed to be a summon spell gem that had turned sentient, even showing the spell gem lodged in its forehead. It claimed that it only wanted to summon more of its kind so it could reproduce but [[Tholraak]] didn't believe him. He turned the slaad over to the people of [[Blingdenstone]], to which they sentenced it to death. Right before being executed, it called him a liar and claimed that [[Fraz-urb-luu]] would love him.
