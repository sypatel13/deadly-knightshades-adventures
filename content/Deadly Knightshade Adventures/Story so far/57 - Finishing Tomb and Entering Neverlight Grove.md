# Finishing Tomb and Entering [[Neverlight Grove]]

[[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] opened the sarcophagus of the underground room to reveal a skeleton and hilt with a shiny jewel on the bottom of it. After picking up the skeleton holding the hilt, it fell out of the skeleton's arms to the bottom of the sarcophagus, making it scream "NOOO not the darkness again\!" [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]] pulled the hilt out, immediately bonding with it. It told us that it had been touched by [[Ra]], that it belonged to the leader of an ancient race/people that would bury valuables with them and has since died off. It hasn't felt as powerful/close to [[Ra]] since being hidden in the tomb. It asked to be named and [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]] chose [[Sparky]]. When asked what he could do, [[Sparky]] shined very bright, blinding[[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]]who was guided by [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] for the rest of the session. On the way out of the tomb, [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] felt called to vanquish the evil he sensed before. The party entered the room that [[Sparky]] called a false tomb. We opened the sarcophagus in there and a giant shade popped out of it. Even though our holiest member couldn't see and [[Kadulus]] was still in shock from hearing "my friend" too much, [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] called on [[Waukeen]] herself to smite the scary man away.

We left the tomb and continued on our way to [[Neverlight Grove]]. On the way, we set up camp next to some tall mushroom trees. When [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] activated his mace, the trees got up and tried to extinguish it. [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]] spoke to the trees to confirm this and all went back to normal after [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] extinguished the mace.   
Two michonids (shroom people) showed up with some corpses and said they were coming back from a hunt, which is just finding dead stuff for them. We asked if they could guide us to [[Neverlight Grove]] and they said sure. We also looted the dead bodies, finding a mysterious potion and a worn out journal. One of them was acting very strange and kept talking about the celebration and wouldn't explain why when asked. We tried to put *[[Kalelgoni]] butt medicine* on him and it burned him but not the other mochonid. On the way, they also explained that there are multiple circles in the city, which are groups that have different roles. They normally worship at any circle but recently they were encouraged to only worship at their home circle by one of their sovereigns/leaders. There was also a new circle recently established, which is the leadership circle.   
We made it to [[Neverlight Grove]], where we were introduced to 2 sovereigns, [[Phylo]] and [[Basidia]]. Phylo was acting like the other weird shroom, but [[Basidia]] offered to give us a tour of the city, which we agreed to. On the way, she noted that the other sovereign as well as other shrooms had been acting strange and asked for our help investigating. She brought us to the hunter's circle and introduced us to [[Loomaub]], who explained how they "hunt" and asked us to kill a Grick Alpha and a Shambling Mound, as they had been giving the hunting michonids a hard time. We agreed to get back to his request later. He expressed the same concern for crazy michonids as [[Basidia]]. We continued the tour and ended here.  
We continued from The Hunter's Circle to the Explorer's Circle. We met the leader of the Explorer's Circle there who marked a path to Blindenstone on our map, warning us that there were corpses of a druidic cult potentially worshiping [[Orcus]]. We said thank you and he said to repay him by purging the land of weirdness. [[Basidia]] informed us that anyone else she introduced us to has been acting strangely. We made our way to the nursery, where we were greeted by a dancing michonid. They said two michonids can share spores and plant a baby one, which they take care of. We stopped by the Alchemists Circle, where [[Basidia]] said she/they came from and explained most of [[Neverlight Grove]]'s income is from trading potions to other regions. We continued to the Builder's Circle where we met Bob the Builder. He was extremely focused on a miniature model of a building. When we approached, he acknowledged that we were from the topside and asked what our buildings were like. Upon hearing about stairs, he went "Aha\!" and became extremely focused on the model. [[Quackity]] wafted some *Kalelgoni medicine* in his direction, making his skin boil, although he seemed to not care at all. [[Basidia]] witnessed what happened, pulled us aside, and asked for a sample of the medicine. She said she could try to reverse engineer it but that it would take a very long time.
