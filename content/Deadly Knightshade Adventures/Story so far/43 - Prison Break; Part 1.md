# Prison Break; Part 1

[[Kadulus]] woke up being thrown into a cell in nothing but his undies. He was sent to dish duty and met a small man named [[Bupido]] claiming to be a god. He also decided to kick the chair of [[Ilvara]], earning him 2 or 3 beatings. He was woken up to a thicc dummy aka [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] being thrown on him. They didn’t have long to talk before [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] was put on barrel duty, filling barrels with water. He met an elf named [[Prince Dendril]] who claimed to be polymorphed into a quaggoth by a witch. That night [[Quackity]] and [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]] were thrown into the cell then put on lift duty. They went with a dwarf lady named [[Edith]] and deep gnome named [[Jim Jar]] to lower barrels beneath the spider webs. They talked for a bit then a drow commander named [[Jorlan]] hinted that he was interested in making [[Ilvara]] look bad for replacing him as a lover with another drow named [[Shore]].  
Later at lunch a mushroom was pushed by an orc for its food. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] and [[Quackity]] stepped in, but then [[Ilvara]] broke it up with [[Quackity]]'s tentacle wand, feeding orc to spiders. [[Tholraak]] joined the group in the cell, was assigned toilet cleaning duty, where he met a fish man named [[Shushar The Awoken]] and a manic drow named [[Sareth]]. In the middle of planning how they would escape, demons attacked the prison. [[Jorlan]] used this excuse to open the cell doors. The party fought some drow in the armory and picked up some weapons/armor. They went to the royal quarters and found some of their stuff and then some in fancy chests.
