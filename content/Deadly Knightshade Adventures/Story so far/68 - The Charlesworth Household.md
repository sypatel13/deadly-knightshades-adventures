# The Charlesworth Household

After a night of celebrating, the party woke up at The Drunken Mug. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] quickly went over to [[Gernick Tapfinger]] to ask about the soul bag, but didn’t have any advice to offer. He returned to the rest of the party and we got ready to leave.  
We departed [[Blingdenstone]], leaving behind [[Pebbles]], [[Mama Bear]], and the lizardfolk. It only took us about a day to find the exit of the Underdark. It was at the top of a very steep hill and the exit was blocked by some collapsed rocks. Because it was so dark, [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]] thought it was a good idea to take [[Sparky|Solais]] (aka [[Sparky]]) out and try to light the way. However, all this accomplished was blinding half the party. We took a second to carefully remove the rocks that blocked our path and used rope to connect the blind to those still able to see.  
Leaving the tunnel, we found ourselves in the middle of a flower field. Describing it to [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]], he realized he was very close to his home. After falling into a creek due to rope difficulties, we ran into a small child that turned out to be [[Chester Charlesworth]]. He led us back to his house but had a ton of questions along the way about who we were and what we were doing here. On the path, we also came across a kitty with golden fur that seemed to like [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]], who proceeded to pick it up.
