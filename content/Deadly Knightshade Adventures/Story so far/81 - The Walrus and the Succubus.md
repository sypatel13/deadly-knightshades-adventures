# The Walrus and the Succubus

The party ran into [[Scruffy]] the walrus and his trusty bird washbucket. He told the party that he had been following them in an effort to bring them back to the overworld so they could fulfill their duties. After explaining how there won’t be an Overworld to tend to if we don’t stop the demon lord apocalypse to Scruffy, he agreed to join Deadly Nightshade since it would be quicker to help than to drag all of us to the surface.  
Walking into the sanctum, the party didn’t hesitate to initiate battle. Malcanthet utilized her Phalace (obelisk) of [[Graz'zt]] to summon demon helpers, including lesser succubi, giant apes, spiky doods, and a very confused giant octopus. [[Kadulus]] hasted Gildo, who hasted Luna and flew recklessly towards Malcanthet and her succubi with a vengeance. Quackity’s arrows weren’t shootin so good, but that didn’t stop him from trying. Lester, Scruffy, and [[Kadulus]] worked their way down the bridge to get to the obelisk and Malcanthet. As [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] got the life sucked out of him, they evaporated the minions that were summoned.
