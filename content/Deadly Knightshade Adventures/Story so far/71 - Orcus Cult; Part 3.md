# Orcus Cult; Part 3, Slaying Trees

After the party took a short rest from the horrors they had witnessed in the dungeon so far, we explored the room we were in. We found notes talking about experimentation and mentioning "\[4 somethings become one\]". We also found a wand made of a spine with a skull atop it as described in the notes on [[Orcus]], but a little different/lesser as it wasn't in pristine condition and seemed like a remake. We decided to give it to [[Kadulus]] as it felt good in his hands but stinky/weird to everyone else. Continuing through the double doors, we found a hallway, which [[Quackity]] found another secret hallway in.   
We decided to split the party with [[Tholraak]], [[Luna]], [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]], and some skeletons going one way and [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]], [[Quackity]], [[Kadulus]], and skeletons going another way. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] casted *Haste* on [[Quackity]] and we caught the tree fucks by surprise. There were four lesser wooden bois originally, but as the fight went on a big wooden Ent came out of the giant tree in the middle of the room. The rest of the party dealt with the small trees while [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] poked at the big one's toe with his sword and got knocked on his ass repeatedly by its gargantuan smacks. Throughout the battle, [[Kadulus]] felt the wand demand him to siphon life and deal as much damage as possible to the trees, putting his own life at risk in the process. [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]] was a holy man and healed the party a ton in the middle of the fight, saving [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] and [[Luna]] from the brink of wooden death. He also increased [[Tholraak]]'s strength for shits and giggles. [[Kadulus]] sacrificed many skeletons for the good of the party. [[Quackity]] fired a million arrows, utilizing the *Haste*. Once the big tree had taken enough damidge, it's chest opened up to reveal a dragon's heart. We focused on it until it was destroyed.   
Afterwards, the party took a second to catch their breath. [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]] sat upon the stump, [[Quackity]] sacrificed the dragon heart to his god, [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] praised [[Waukeen]] (which [[Luna]] approved of, suck it [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]]), and [[Kadulus]] gathered the bones of [[Rancanzor]]. On the way out, [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] thought to set the mural of [[Orcus]] ablaze. Seeing this, [[Kadulus]]' new strange wand commanded him to shoot a blast of necrotic energy at [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] for max damage. [[Tholraak]] and [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]] stood there shocked while [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] and [[Quackity]] wrestled it out of his hands. Upon leaving the dungeon, we came across a small flame fox. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] began to pet it, but then it opened its mouth and a voice said "Is this thing on? Adventurers, you are needed in [[Gauntlgrym]], what do you call yourselves?" to which [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] replied ***Deadly Knightshade***™️, then it scampered off at the speed of light, leaving behind a trail of fire.
