# Orcus Cult; Part 1, Bad Vibes

The party made their way back into the Underdark to a druidic cult to [[Orcus]] they had heard about. They were able to arrive in just a day's time. We were instead met with a giant tree at the exact spot that was marked on their map. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]]'s divine sense could tell the tree was good but what was below it was evil. Using speak with plants, [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]] was able to discover that it was guarding a great evil below. We convinced it that we were a group of good and would vanquish such evil. The tree opened up and we repelled down on a rope. There was a room with bad juju that we realized was used as a sacrificial chamber. There was a shattered gem and markings on the wall but we were too creeped out to tell what they were for. Going deeper into the dungeon, we found a room with a bunch of books. Most of them were about the seggs, burning [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]]'s hands to the touch. But others had information about [[Orcus]], including his rise to power and features.   
Orcus book notes: 

- Mortal  
- Dark Sorcerer  
- Banished to the 113th layer of the abyss  
- Rose up becoming the master of the 113th layer  
- Prince of Undead  
- Orcus created the wand of Orcus that he used to break havoc but it was destroyed by [[Garreth Dragonsbane]]. Orcus' followers are waiting for him and the wand  
- The wand of Orcus is a humanoid skull mounted atop a spinal column. Depicted with a red aura. Generally used as a symbol for him  
- At the peak of Orcus' power, he was enemies with the [[Graz'zt]] and [[Demogorgon]]  
- Orcus considers anyone who gets in the way his mortal enemy.

We also found an [[Orcus]] statue that commanded most of the party to bow but [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]]. [[Tholraak]] and [[Kadulus]] went to investigate down one hallway while the rest of the party investigated some worm things down another hallway. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]], a master of combat, blocked his allies off from fighting the worm things, essentially 1v2ing. Once he realized he was dying (even with two nat 20s), he ran out of the hallway and let his allies do the fighting. After healing himself to full health, [[Luna]] questioned his healing and [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]] was a cock about it as well. We continued to a big room with the sounds of something devouring a creature. It turned out to be a shambling mound, which we did away with quickly but found nothing else of note in the room.
