# 
[[Shaw]] picks up a Stone Speaker Crystal then heads to the Perfect Dimension and explore there. She notices the intricate pipework and wants to study perfection. He studies the pipes and see a B-trap. He starts to get an idea but is a little out of his depth. He looks into his Stone Speaker Crystal to look at books. There are several books with several titles. 

[[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] tries to locate [[Noah the Boa]] and asks, “Do you have a specialist for locating objects and/or souls?”. [[Noah the Boa]] thinks about it for a second and mentions [[Thrumgar]] would be the closest expert. [[Noah the Boa]] asks for more clarifying questions on what [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] is attempting to accomplish. He says he wants to talk to a soul he hasn’t talk to in a long time but also he wants to locate the [[Holy Avenger]] for it’s uniquely amazing combat ability for their future fights. [[Noah the Boa]] mentions [[Ogrm Slateheart]] may be able to help with the [[Holy Avenger]] but souls are a bit harder but an expert on their plane could help. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] uses his Crystal to look for [[Brother Varknack|Varknack]]. [[Brother Varknack|Varknack]] calls [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] out for being a Celestial and he retorts about [[Brother Varknack|Varknack]] is an expert on [[Plane of Arcadia]]. [[Brother Varknack|Varknack]] asks about what’s in [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]]’s pockets and [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] brings up [[Kalelgoni]] and the Deck of some things. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] looks closer at the scroll paper. He notices it says “Owner of Castle Stormborn”. [[Brother Varknack|Varknack]] asks [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] to unroll the scroll. He does so and sees his reflection in the scroll. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] asks why is his face in the paper and [[Brother Varknack|Varknack]] leans over to look into it and his face isn’t reflected. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] asks if this is how Celestials sign papers. [[Brother Varknack|Varknack]] explains that the Stormborn’s has a connection to that castle and the last living heir will be reflected in the paper. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] mentions that this is an unfortunate way to learn that Drogo is dead. He didn’t realize he travelled that far so long ago and asks how to get there. [[Brother Varknack|Varknack]] says he’s been trying to setup a portal but the castle is invaded. 

Paning back to [[Shaw]], he spent half the day looking through books skimming chapter titles to find information that is relevant to his pursuits. A large portion of the texts are in common as a form of record keeping. This reminds [[Shaw]] of illusion magic that is affecting the fluids in the pipes. 

[[Kadulus]] finds a nice corner to setup his study space and looks into his Crystal for spells. After a sufficient time, [[Kadulus]] feels out of his depth on his study of dimensions. He sets out to seek help on understanding this information further. [[Kadulus]] sets off to speak to [[Zundrak of The Broken Peak|Zundrak]]. After a jump, skip, and a hop, he finds himself in the Astral Plane. [[Kadulus]] starts to ADHD ramble at [[Zundrak of The Broken Peak|Zundrak]] until [[Zundrak of The Broken Peak|Zundrak]] silences him telling him and tells him to listen. Urging him to think of his memories of memories. [[Kadulus]] meditates and has a Hugo-esque flashback to Kadgalf. In another flashback, he is an elf sitting hand in hand with his lover with a gentle breeze. In his final flashback, he is an orc hurting his food on the head of a fallen comrade. [[Kadulus]] returns to and has a deeper understanding of dimensions he was reading about.

[[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] thanks [[Brother Varknack|Varknack]] for his help and heads off to speak to [[Ogrm Slateheart]] about the [[Holy Avenger]]. [[Ogrm Slateheart]] mentions he knows very little of the [[Holy Avenger]]. It was lost in the 3rd invasion of [[Menzobranzen]]. It was lost at some point while invade through Donigarten -> The Slums of The Braeryn -> Up the shit pipe -> to Tier Breche to execute the general. [[Kadulus]] returns exhausted but stops to help [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] to help him and realizes that the [[Holy Avenger]] is at the bottom of the Donigarten. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] thanks [[Ogrm Slateheart]] as a couple portals open dropping [[Shaw]], [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]] and [[Quackity]] back with the group. We sit down for a nice simple lunch of cheese and bread. 

[[Quackity]] tells the group about the story of [[Arushnee]] and [[Mielikki]] as sisters and [[Arushnee]]’s progression to an evil god. He explains her spread of influence and shares his ideas about bring [[Arushnee]] back to her original form as a positive god. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] asks the party if they want to go save his home and reveals that he is not from the Material Plane. [[Quackity]] spends time asking questions about [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]]’s travels and [[Graz'zt]] hatred of his family. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] recalls his Castle is next to the ocean. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] also brings up the deed he got from the Deck of Some Things is the deed to his Castle. [[Quackity]] asks [[Mielikki]] to save us. The party tries to wrap their heads around why we need to go save [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]]’s Castle. [[Kadulus]] goes and gets [[Brother Varknack|Varknack]] about why these two are related. He clarifies that [[Plane of Arcadia]] is a room in the Library that needs to be liberated to learn more about [[Plane of Arcadia]]. [[Quackity]] pulls out his looking glass and [[Brother Varknack|Varknack]] gets excited about it. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] also shares that his father has passed as he is now the heir of Castle Stormborn. 

We travel and make our way to the [[Plane of Arcadia]] and begin to Stormborn the Castle Stormborn.
