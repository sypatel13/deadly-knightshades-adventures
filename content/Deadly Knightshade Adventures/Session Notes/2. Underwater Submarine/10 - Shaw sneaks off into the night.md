# 10 - Shaw sneaks off into the night
[[Shaw]] sneaks off into the night and is called by a giant. [[Shaw]] find himself in the area with young [[Zelron Roaringhorn]] cooking a pecarino. Flavorful smells fill the room and [[Shaw]] notices a group of familiar halflings in the corner. He notices his cousins Thiafret and his wife, she is holding a youngin. The rest of the members look related to [[Shaw]]. The giant refocuses [[Shaw]] as [[Zelron Roaringhorn]] says, “Mamma Mia, It’s a ready” and begins to move the ingredients around. As this happens, [[Shaw]]’s nervous cousin takes a seat at the table. Thiafret tells him to relax but he mutters, “Here comes the bad part.” Thiafret took out a rival family and states, “You think we wouldn’t get to you”. Young [[Shaw]] runs over to Thiafret asking what happened and Thiafret says, “What needed to be done”. [[Zelron Roaringhorn]] runs off into the night and changes his name.

---

[[Shaw]] regale the tale to us the next morning. After he completes, Willow and her siblings show up with the healers. [[Walloper]] tries to balance and falls. Bonker is fitted with a prosthetic eye to help prevent infections. The Kenku and [[Quackity]] discuss about our plans going forward as [[Hgraam Stonespeaker]] approaches to come take the Kenku home. The Kenku said they are staying but [[Quackity]] says we need their help on the home front to protect it from the [[Demon Lords]], they argue back about [[Sibreix]] and [[Quackity]] says that we can’t commit all of our power towards the front force. We need to divide and conquer. [[Walloper]] does a jump off [[Scruffy]] and lands it knocking the wind out of him. The party spends some time hyping up the Kenku until [[Hgraam Stonespeaker]] tells us the time is near. 

We thank him for allowing us to be at the [[Graven Hollow Library]] and [[Hgraam Stonespeaker]] says it’s truly an honor to work here. [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]] begins to name flowers. 
And [[Hgraam Stonespeaker]] opens a portal to let the Kenku and our letters to [[Gauntlgrym]].

[[Hgraam Stonespeaker]] tells us it is time for us to depart as well and [[Noah the Boa]] shows up to escort us out of the library. We are given 18 rations bringing us to a total of 28 rations.

[[Noah the Boa]] begins to guide us out of the library through the perfect dimension into Shadowfell. Everyone is through as [[Kadulus]] lingers as he is greeted from behind. Klaus appears and converses with [[Kadulus]]. He’s capturing souls and selling it to the highest demon lord bidder. He tries to convince [[Kadulus]] to join him where the money is. [[Kadulus]] tells him that, “working with the demon lords is not worth it.” Klaus begs [[Kadulus]] to not fight the [[Demon Lords]] and seek a safe plane. [[Kadulus]] says, ”At least facing death will prove I am alive. I will be doing it for me instead of something someone else wants.” Klaus expresses his disappointment and turns and disappears into Shadowfell.

[[Kadulus]] goes through the portal, asks [[Noah the Boa]] if the Shadowfell portal is current or stuck in the past. [[Noah the Boa]] states that Shadowfell is a present portal. [[Kadulus]] relays to the party the news. We continue to make our way through the portals to return to our ship. [[Noah the Boa]] gets us to our final portal and bids us a farewell. We thank [[Noah the Boa]] for his help and aid during our visit and we hope to see him again.
