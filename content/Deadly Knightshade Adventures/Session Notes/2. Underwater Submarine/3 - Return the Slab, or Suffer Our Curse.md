# Return the Slab, or Suffer Our Curse

## Day 2 (cont.)

[[Qwnt]] is in the unholy room. Everyone else is in the holy room. [[Shaw]] is keeping an eye on [[Qwnt]]. [[Qwnt]] apologizes to [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] for overstepping the chain of command. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] give [[Qwnt]] a history lesson on the Kenku People. [[Qwnt]] flirts with [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] some more. [[Qwnt]] shares some alcohol with the party. Everyone except [[Kadulus]] partake. [[Qwnt]] shares stories of glory, he’s been in the navy and at sea for so long that he doesn’t remember what he’s done before. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] with some help from the party regales our adventures until [[Qwnt]] takes over mentioning all his scars and how he got them. 

[[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]] asks [[Qwnt]] about his square scar. [[Qwnt]] says that the scar is from a tattoo he had removed related to the ship he sailed, GRN Obsidian. [[Qwnt]] tells us the harrowing story of how his ship went down and the 170 men on the ship went into the water. 170 men went into the water… and the Kraken began to toy with them. One-by-one, the Kraken picked them off. Ripping then away from their lives, their futures, their family. 170 men went into the water…. 35 men came out. 

[[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]] and [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] express their sympathy for [[Qwnt]] and his hatred of Krakens. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] wants to find compromise as he explains we wish to help [[Qwnt]] but the [[Demon Lords]] take priority as they threaten safety of all of the Underdark. 

[[Quackity]] asks [[Qwnt]] about if he has any knowledge of a lost Library owned and operated by Giants. We hope to find more information there about the [[Demon Lords]]. [[Quackity]] also asks if [[Qwnt]] has any knowledge of the Holy Avenger. 

The Party rests for the evening

**DAYS TIL VOTE** - *18*

## Day 3

[[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]] calls for [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] to move the ship. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] calls to [[Quackity]] to move the ship. We head South West attempting to follow the tentacle. We lost sight of the tentacle in the Kelp Forest. [[Qwnt]] mentions turbulent waters ahead. We press forward and adjust course as we spot signs of the tentacles towards the North West. [[Qwnt]] states we should have been out of the Kelp Forest by now. We find ourselves by a sunken ship. [[Quackity]] sends out a sonar pulse to get a lay of the land before disembarking to explore the ship. We find out there are creatures towards the North towards the edge of our sonar pulse and towards the surface. [[Quackity]], [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]], and [[Shaw]] disembark to explore the sunken cargo ship. The exploration group finds a gem polished into a perfect oval (50gp) and a corpse of a dead rat (Has squiggly stink lines).  

**DAYS TIL VOTE** - *17*

We are awoken by some strange human people who can swim underwater. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] has a conversation with one of them at the main looking glass. The strange human messages [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] and introduces himself as [[Seastar]]. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] invites them onto our ship to discuss their cause. [[Seastar]] tries to get [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] into an MLM. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] almost falls for it and tell [[Seastar]] to take a hike. [[Seastar]] starts to get hostile. 

We beat up [[Seastar]] and his goons.
