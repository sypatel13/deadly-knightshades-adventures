# 12 - Shaw finds the Holy Avenger
[[Kadulus]] collects some goop of [[Demogorgon]] and [[Quackity]] lets [[Qwnt]] know that we won and survived. After a quick rest, [[Kadulus]] gathers everyone up to teleport us and the ship to the Cheese Lake in [[Menzobranzen]]. After much effort we succeed and find ourselves in the Cheese Lake. At this point, [[Quackity]] remembers that [[Qwnt]] is in enemy territory with a stolen ship. We move around searching the bottom of the lake attempting to find the Holy Avenger. [[Quackity]] pulls one of the coffins in to investigate further.
182 - The corpse is holding a broom. It’s a good fucking broom
[[Quackity]], [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]], [[Scruffy]], [[Shaw]], and [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]] swim out into the water to hunt for the Holy Avenger
438 - A wand sticking out of a broken coffin
[[Shaw]] sees a shiny gold bit sticking out of a coffin as he pulls out a Holy Avenger. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] drolls while managing to hold his shit in. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] gets tingly when he touches it. It lights up the lake with a faint angelic hum. 
[[Qwnt]] begins to question where we are and begins to get upset about us bringing him to the Drow Capital [[Menzobranzen]]. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] and [[Quackity]] argue about what to do with the ship. [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]] asks [[Kadulus]] what the wand does that he found and after discovering that is creates pillows, we all retire to long rest. [[Kadulus]] teleports us to the bottom of the dark lake, we relinquish the ship to [[Qwnt]] and make our way to the stealth rock to make our way into [[Menzobranzen]] once again.
We make it into the city proper and notice that the docks are hussling and bussling as folks seem a bit dazed from the tentacle influences has been removed. We make our way through the forest path and encounter a wild spider wishing for food. We give it some kraken meat and make our way to the Arachne Ovum.
We run into the house quarters for Del’Armgo as a crowd is forming to hear an announcement. A woman enters onto the platform. She has a commanding figure with a strong jaw line. We hear a magically enhanced voice say, “Quiet for Matron Mother, Del’Armgo”. She announces that there it has come to her attention that has been a declaration of divine recognition. We want to rest assure that House Del’Armgo will not vote yes to such a treasonous declaration. Their allies have also agreed to not allow such a declaration pass. Long Live the Crucible of the Spider Queen. Long Live House Del’Armgo. The crowd cheers. As she finishes her speech and exits, the crowd erupts in conversations. 
We make our way to the Archane Ovum and head to the bar to rent a room for the evening. After we rent our room, we make our way upstairs. [[Kadulus]] notices that we are being watched intently by what seems like a average Jane. We begin to discuss the Squash Buckets need for french fries. We lock up the room and head out towards [[UMD]] to meet up with [[Zszrak Freiryn|Zszrak]]. We cut through the Bazaar in hopes of losing any people tailing us. 
As we move through the Bazaar, the gossip is spreading about House Del’Armgo made an announcement about the declaration and the scare of the demon lords. We make our way up to Tier Breache and see a large amount of drow worshiping [[Lolth]]. We head towards the [[UMD]] and we make our way to the counter which is manned by [[Zszrak Freiryn|Zszrak]]. [[Kadulus]] flashes the older library card and [[Zszrak Freiryn|Zszrak]] immediately recognizes what is happening and pulls us into a room. 
We exchange information with [[Zszrak Freiryn|Zszrak]] and ask him for help to hunt down the [[Chud Magician]]. [[Zszrak Freiryn|Zszrak]] tells us his office atop of the Narbondel Tower. The meeting is being help tomorrow which is held at the top of Narbondel Tower. 
We head to a dive bar in an attempt to get information. [[Quackity]], [[Kadulus]], and [[Scruffy]] overhear various conversation about the goings ons. [[Quackity]] found out about the Octaid, [[Kadulus]] learns more about the Crucible, and [[Scruffy]] learns about the [[Chud Magician]]’s wife.
