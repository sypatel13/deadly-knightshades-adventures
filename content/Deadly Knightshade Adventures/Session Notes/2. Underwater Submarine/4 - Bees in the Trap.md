**DAYS TIL VOTE** - *17*
**Ration Count** - 32
### Day 4
[[Shaw]] has inspiration. After a restless night, we start our day foraging for rations. The exploration team recovered 4 rations for us. 

Based on Foraging Difficulty
- Easy Forage - 16 DC
- Medium Forage - 18 DC
- Hard Forage - 20 DC

[[Quackity]] brings up concerns of our timeline. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] asks [[Qwnt]] if there is a way for us to understand what our baring are. [[Quackity]] mentions surfacing to figure out where we are. The party discusses which way to go; debating surfacing, going south, or going north. The party agreed to go west at a forced pace hoping we get out of the Kelp Forest. [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]] after much meditation decides to cast “Guiding Boat” to give [[Quackity]] advantage on navigating the boat. After some trial and tribulations, we find our wait to open calm waters. [[Kadulus]], as we get to the edge of the kelp forest, meets a glowing red pair of eyes. The owner of the eyes is attempting to hide. [[Kadulus]] alerts [[Quackity]] to the red eyes. We decide to keep it to ourselves. [[Quackity]] spots the tentacle coming out of the Kelp Forest heading in the North West direction. We pursue. The Bots alert [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] that we near capacity for structural integrity to traverse this section of the lake. We slingshot on the whirlpool putting us next to the tentacle. We recover 3 EU. 

**DAYS TIL VOTE** - 16
**Ration Count** - 28

### Day 5
After resting, we get setup to move deciding to follow the tentacle North West. We start moving at a forced pace and find ourselves in a couple of whirlpools. We lose the tentacles a couple times and then in a very ominous manner, we see this enormous whirlpool, stretching as far as we can see. We attempt to get out of it but it sucks us in, landing us at the bottom at the eye of the storm. This is perfect stable conditions based on The Bots. It seems we have found a safe haven. We found a brownish crystal gemstone (50gp) and an enormous wheel of cheese. Increases our rations by 9. 

**DAYS TIL VOTE** - 15
**Ration Count** - 29

### Day 6
We begin our journey for the day towards the South West at a Forced Pace. [[Quackity]] leads us to victory! We run into some stalagmites taking 75 damage but [[Quackity]] rights the ship and we make send ourselves South West. We settle into a cravace.

**DAYS TIL VOTE** - 14
**Ration Count** - 21

### Day 7
Coming out of this camp, we head towards the North West at a normal pace with intentions to forage for food. We find ourselves in a pickle but [[Quackity]] with the quickness navigates us however the ship sustains 50 damage. After the ship settles at the bottom and we find some goodies. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] finds a shield with a bee emblem on it, [[Quackity]] finds, in the rubble, a perfectly carved figure of a raven made of Basalt (25gp), [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]] finds a quiver of 12 pristine +3 arrows, and [[Kadulus]] finds a magical time-keeping device produced by an artisan. Opening this circular locket allows you to check the current time based on where a delicate hand is pointing. On one half of the circular face is an ornate night's sky with a crescent moon indicating midnight. On the other half is a blue sky, with a bright sun indicating mid-day. It's unclear how the magic has been infused with the device, as no arcane presence is detectable, but pulling the silver panel off the back of the locket simply exposes a number of delicate, ticking gears. The party converges on and finds a pile of coal. 
[[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] begins to mess around with the shield. He discovers a reflector shield that converts ranged attacks into a swarm of attacking bees. 
[[Kadulus]] examines his time piece and feels some innate magical ability but can’t quite put it in words. He feels as if it connects him to his brother. 
[[Quackity]] tries to assess the nature of his statue and does a self-inflicted racism.
The turbulent waters cause us to not find any rations to bolster our reserves.

**DAYS TIL VOTE** - 13
**Ration Count** - 13

### Day 8
We begin navigating towards the North West. We travel a full day and we are still in the whirlpool. [[Quackity]] hunts down a large cache of rations bring us to 26 rations total. 

**DAYS TIL VOTE** - 12

### Day 9
[[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]] guides the boat to aid [[Quackity]] in navigating the ship towards the tentacle. [[Kadulus]] notices the patterns in the current and relays the information to [[Quackity]]. We slingshot using the whirlpool and take 48 damage. We get ourselves into calmer waters. We continue forwards into calmer waters as we track the tentacle. 
[[Kadulus]] notices a creature trying to sneak in from the belly of our ship and alerts the party to it’s presence.
