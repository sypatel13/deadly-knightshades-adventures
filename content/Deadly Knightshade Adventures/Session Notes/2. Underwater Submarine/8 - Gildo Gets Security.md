# 8 - 
[[Scruffy]] makes the prodigal return. He awakens on the [[Hexcrawl Ship]] and is handed a tuning fork by [[Qwnt]]. [[Scruffy]] attempts to get his baring and utilized his tuning fork to re-join the party. 
[[Scruffy]] runs approaches a beast and is stopped by a pack of Leonin that helped show him the ways of Pack fighting. [[Scruffy]] with the Leonin work together to destroy the beast and he continues his journey forward and runs into a Stone Giant. [[Scruffy]] continues forward and runs into a young [[Zelron Roaringhorn]]. 

[[Scruffy]] arrives to aid the party in storming the castle and delivers the killing blow to Green Jesus. The party exclaims how [[Scruffy]] got here. [[Scruffy]] regales that tale above to the party. 

The party heads into the castle to explore. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] stops as he remembers where his sister laid, slain. He continues poking around and finds a rod. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] picks it up, fighting back tears and he is reminded of his family. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] inspects it further and finds an engraving on it in his father’s script saying, “Gildo I hope you find security one day”. 

We get back to the main room of the library. [[Scruffy]] took a plate of fish sticks to the hot tub to chill. We all hang out in the hot tub taking turns falling asleep.
