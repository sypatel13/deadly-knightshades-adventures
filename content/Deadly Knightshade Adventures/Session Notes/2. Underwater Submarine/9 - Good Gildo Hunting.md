# 9 - Good Gildo Hunting
[[Shaw]] sneaks off in the night. We gather up in the next day for our breakfast. [[Brother Varknack|Varknack]] approaches us and mentions that they’ve gotten the timewarp setup. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] asks if we can all see it and [[Brother Varknack|Varknack]] confirms that it’s similar to the room containing young [[Zelron Roaringhorn]]. [[Hrogath]] joins us and mentions a plain with someone looking for [[Luna]], mentions his name is [[Bahamut]]. [[Luna]] gives us a fairwell and [[Hrogath]] wacks his tuning fork and a portal opens with a green dragonborn who smiles at us. [[Luna]] hops into the portal and [[Tholraak]] smiles at us as the portal closes. 

After our emotional departure, we continue eating our breakfast. [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]] didn’t notice due to the oatmeal. [[Hgraam Stonespeaker]] comes over and mentions, “You are welcome to stay as long as you need. But please remember, if you stay too long out of time you will be hurt when you leave. You have a day or two at most.” [[Hgraam Stonespeaker]] pulls out a map to show us the [[Darklake]] and point to where he suspects [[Sibreix]] to be located. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] asks [[Hgraam Stonespeaker]] questions about how the Drow have learned to open portals to Mechanis and various other plains and if it’s similar to the tuning forks. [[Hgraam Stonespeaker]] says the forks allow for little jumps whereas the Drow have opened the warehouse doors. How they have achieved it is still beyond the understand of the Stone Giants. 

[[Quackity]] looks [[Ogran]] to learn about recent events. [[Quackity]] mentions that he may have made a mistake in the recent past. He mentions the prisoners of the Drow prison and wants to know about the inmates. [[Ogran]] mentions that there are a variety of levels. [[Quackity]] begins to explain the various locks and powers that he had around him. [[Ogran]] gets concerned and asks if it was a Mindflayer. [[Quackity]] is confused and [[Ogran]] pulls out a parchment showing his face. [[Quackity]] confirms that his and [[Ogran]] mentions that his name is [[Xal'Zharthun]]

[[Ogran]] explains that the Mindflayer is the sworn enemy of the Drow. One Mindflayer will destroy all of Drow Society. [[Quackity]] asks about stopping [[Xal'Zharthun]] and [[Ogran]] mentions that it took the Drow millenia to stop, capture, and attempt to kill [[Xal'Zharthun]].

We make our way to the [[Plane of Arcadia]]. Along the way, [[Kadulus]] stops in Shadowfell to look off into the distance. We continue on and [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] notices a familiar black gem and picks it up. Suddenly, a black horse appears and announcing that is will aid us in the near future. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] thanks it and we move on to [[Plane of Arcadia]]. We arrive and [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] begins to notice all the beauties of the castle as it has been reverse through times. 

[[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]]’s sister, Luna, begins to grill him with a billion questions. The party observes [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]]’s interactions with his sister and keep there distance. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] keeps talking about a hunting trip. 

We make our way to the web dimensions and run into [[Balmar]]. [[Balmar]] tells us that his job is to listen to what the web say. [[Quackity]] asks about the room before which is a room of pure evil. [[Balmar]] refocuses us to listen to the web for the will of [[Arushnee]]. She’s gathering power and sewing seeds of chaos throughout the lands.

The Party made a stop in Sky Sweden and [[Quackity]] attempted to spread the influence of [[Mielikki]] to Thor and Odin
