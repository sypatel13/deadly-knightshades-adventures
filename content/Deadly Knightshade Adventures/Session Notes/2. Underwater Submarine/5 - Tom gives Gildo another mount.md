### Day 10
[[Quackity]] leads us deep into the lake and we make some huge progress. The party forages and finds 6 additional rations bringing us to 17 rations. While begin to settle in for the night, [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] goes our and finds a creature that seems to suck on objects and clean them so he bring it around to the main window to show [[Kadulus]]. [[Kadulus]] notices that it’s seems relatively friends and [[Quackity]] confirms this later. [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]] is talking to the Kenku about artificial appendages. [[Quackity]] wants to make the creature into rations and discusses it with [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]]. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] spills in to the ship and mentions it’s an underwater mount. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] claims it can lead us to more mushrooms and [[Quackity]] checks if the mushrooms are safe. [[Lester Charlesworth|Lester]] sends [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] out to gather more and the creature gets under [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] as a mount. It speeds off to take [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] on a ride. [[Gildo Stormborn|Gildo]] names this mysterious large fish-like creature [[Tuna]]. We find a nice valley to rest for the evening. It’s a short rest valley.

**DAYS TIL VOTE** - 10
**Ration Count** - 8

### Day 11
We break down camp to begin moving. [[Quackity]] hunts for rations and we find 4 rations bringing us to a total for 12 rations. [[Tuna]] cleaned the ship while we hunted for rations. We decide to go at a normal pace. We follow the tentacles due South West. It goes on and on and takes a sudden drop as a large endless chasm opens up below us. [[Quackity]] calls to us to buckle up and points the ship straight down. We go down, down, down until we hit the bottom. As we make our way along, we find a geothermal vent which [[Quackity]] deftly dodges it and we continue on. We rest for the evening excited to tackle the chasm.

**DAYS TIL VOTE** - 9
