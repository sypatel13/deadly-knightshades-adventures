# Darklake
- A Lake, but dark  
- Not just one lake, but a huge expanse of many bodies of water linked together in a labyrinth of caves, rivers, lakes, lagoons, etc  
- [[Shushar The Awoken]] has a vague idea of the Layout, as he has been a hermit around the dark lake for much of his life.  
- Very dangerous, many hazards
