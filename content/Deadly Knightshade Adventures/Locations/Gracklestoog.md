# Gracklestoog
- Across the [[Darklake]] from [[Sloobledop]]  
- Biggest city in the Underdark   
- Central trade hub of the Underdark   
- They have LOTS of weapons there, can get any weapon you can think of at the "Blade Bazar"   
- [[Bupido]] is very eager to go there for unclear reasons   
- [[JimJar]] is interested in stopping there insofar as it's a segway to going to [[Blingdenstone]]   
- [[Sarith]] is ok with stopping there insofar as it's a segway to [[Neverlight Grove]]   
- [[Edith]] / [[Prince Dendril|Dendril]] don't care, just want to get out of the Underdark
