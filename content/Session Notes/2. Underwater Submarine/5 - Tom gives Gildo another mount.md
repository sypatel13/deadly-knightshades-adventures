### Day 10

[Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) leads us deep into the lake and we make some huge progress. The party forages and finds 6 additional rations bringing us to 17 rations. While begin to settle in for the night, [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) goes our and finds a creature that seems to suck on objects and clean them so he bring it around to the main window to show [Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md). [Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md) notices that it’s seems relatively friends and [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) confirms this later. [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) is talking to the Kenku about artificial appendages. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) wants to make the creature into rations and discusses it with [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md). [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) spills in to the ship and mentions it’s an underwater mount. [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) claims it can lead us to more mushrooms and [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) checks if the mushrooms are safe. [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) sends [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) out to gather more and the creature gets under [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) as a mount. It speeds off to take [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) on a ride. [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) names this mysterious large fish-like creature *Tuna*. We find a nice valley to rest for the evening. It’s a short rest valley.

**DAYS TIL VOTE** - 10
**Ration Count** - 8

### Day 11

We break down camp to begin moving. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) hunts for rations and we find 4 rations bringing us to a total for 12 rations. *Tuna* cleaned the ship while we hunted for rations. We decide to go at a normal pace. We follow the tentacles due South West. It goes on and on and takes a sudden drop as a large endless chasm opens up below us. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) calls to us to buckle up and points the ship straight down. We go down, down, down until we hit the bottom. As we make our way along, we find a geothermal vent which [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) deftly dodges it and we continue on. We rest for the evening excited to tackle the chasm.

**DAYS TIL VOTE** - 9
**Ration Count** - 3
