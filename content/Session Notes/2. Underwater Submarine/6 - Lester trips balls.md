### Day 12 (Cont.)

[Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) has a conversation with [Noah the Boa](..\NPCs\Noah%20the%20Boa.md) and invites him into the ship. [Noah the Boa](..\NPCs\Noah%20the%20Boa.md) slithers into the ship and apologizes for scaring us. He explains his disguise is to help protect the library. [Noah the Boa](..\NPCs\Noah%20the%20Boa.md) explains how he is the Steward and the giants run the library. They’ve been expecting us. [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) asks about how we get into the library. [Noah the Boa](..\NPCs\Noah%20the%20Boa.md) says we use a planar key to shift into the library’s dimension. We settle to the bottom where we see a large rock that weighs several thousand tons. [Noah the Boa](..\NPCs\Noah%20the%20Boa.md) taps on his planar key which seems like a tuning fork and makes a sound which opens a portal. We enter into the portal and we reach the *Water Dimension*. [Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md) asks about materials to repair the ship but [Noah the Boa](..\NPCs\Noah%20the%20Boa.md) urges us forward. The Stone Giants are expecting us and will explain all. We enter into the second portal and step into the formal entryway. With a wave of his tuning form, we enter into a chilly place. [Noah the Boa](..\NPCs\Noah%20the%20Boa.md) explains how this connects the Water Plane to the Air Plane. -> Beast Lands (100% Humidity Lands/Jungle Lands). *reference Tom’s Document*

* Gildo puts gold back in the gold dimension
* Lester takes some mushrooms from the farplane and decides to collect plants from everywhere we go
* Kadulus has a flashback to his childhood in Shadowfell
* Quackity is babysitting Lester

We finally arrive at the library and are met by [Hgraam Stonespeaker](..\NPCs\Hgraam%20Stonespeaker.md). We ask how they found [Noah the Boa](..\NPCs\Noah%20the%20Boa.md) and he mentions they found him in Elysion. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) asks if the Stone Giants are being hunted. [Hgraam](..\NPCs\Hgraam%20Stonespeaker.md) confirms and states the great powers of evil are always after them. We ask about the demon lords and [Hgraam](..\NPCs\Hgraam%20Stonespeaker.md) says they aren’t a great power. [Hgraam](..\NPCs\Hgraam%20Stonespeaker.md) says we must speak to [Zundrak of The Broken Peak](..\NPCs\Zundrak%20of%20The%20Broken%20Peak.md), the elder, he is in the library. We decide to meet the [Zundrak](..\NPCs\Zundrak%20of%20The%20Broken%20Peak.md) before we settle in to rest and recuperate. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) asks about our looking glass and we find out it looks into a different plane, specifically the *Plane of Arcadia*, being researched by *Brother Varknack*.

We pass through a perfect dimension and when we leave everyone but [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) loses his mind from the lack of perfect level-ness. [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) continues to attempt to each random plants and starts tripping balls. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) reprimands him and then [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) also eats a random mushroom and starts tripping balls. We finally arrive to the final dimension where we meet [Zundrak](..\NPCs\Zundrak%20of%20The%20Broken%20Peak.md). [Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md) enters first and sees the most beautiful array of stars he’s ever seen. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) ties ropes around [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) and [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) who are high as shit. This place leaves us completely at peace. We lack our magical powers but we are still strong and our minds reach a state of peace not ordinarily reached. [Zundrak](..\NPCs\Zundrak%20of%20The%20Broken%20Peak.md) begins to explains the gravity of our problems. He brings up diagram depicting the Material plain, above it the Feywild and below it, Shadowfell. Surround the three are the primal elements; Fire, Water, Air, Earth. [Zundrak](..\NPCs\Zundrak%20of%20The%20Broken%20Peak.md) states, beyond that, there are even more (See Below)

![image.png](..\Files\image.png)

[Zundrak](..\NPCs\Zundrak%20of%20The%20Broken%20Peak.md) explains that this is being represented that our minds can comprehend. The universe is ever large and expanding. We aren’t the center of the universe. The Material Plane might seem like it’s the center however it is more a swirling tapestry of interconnected energies. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) asks how the 6 of us would be capable of dealing with a problem on this scale. [Zundrak](..\NPCs\Zundrak%20of%20The%20Broken%20Peak.md) explains that we are great in power. Greater than even we can believe. He mentions that our souls have been apart of the universe for a long time. [Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md) asks if we have existed a long time and have forgotten. [Zundrak](..\NPCs\Zundrak%20of%20The%20Broken%20Peak.md) says they are all us and it is a matter of perspective. He begins to posit that we are apart of the fundamental forces of good in the universe. We are destined to meet each other and embark on our journeys. [Zundrak](..\NPCs\Zundrak%20of%20The%20Broken%20Peak.md) mentions that the problem we are dealing with is a result of The Abyss pouring into The Material Plane. This is caused by a great evil force attempting to cause this stating that [Chud Magician](..\NPCs\Chud%20Magician.md) is simply a pawn in this game. [Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md) begins to question if we need to stop the [Demon Lords](..\NPCs\Demon%20Lords\Demon%20Lords.md) or stop the greater forces of evil that resulted in the [Demon Lords](..\NPCs\Demon%20Lords\Demon%20Lords.md). [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) asks [Zundrak](..\NPCs\Zundrak%20of%20The%20Broken%20Peak.md) about the Stone Giants philosophy and he responds that their goal is balance. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) follows up by asking if good becomes too powerful would the Giants go to the side of evil. [Zundrak](..\NPCs\Zundrak%20of%20The%20Broken%20Peak.md) responds that if good becomes too powerful to the point of domination, then wouldn’t it be evil. [Zundrak](..\NPCs\Zundrak%20of%20The%20Broken%20Peak.md) urges Quackity to meditate in the room under a tree on the hill then says we need to rest.

[Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) asks about their gods and the lack of [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md). [Zundrak](..\NPCs\Zundrak%20of%20The%20Broken%20Peak.md) explains that [Ra](..\NPCs\Gods\Ra.md) is a core power that surrounds all of us, however, [Waukeen](..\NPCs\Gods\Waukeen.md) is more specific and is under more distinct pressures from the forces of evil. [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) then asks about plants to expand the business and [Zundrak](..\NPCs\Zundrak%20of%20The%20Broken%20Peak.md) refers [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) to the plant expert, *Korval*.

[Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) mentions to [Noah the Boa](..\NPCs\Noah%20the%20Boa.md) that the meeting was as grand as explained. [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) asks about Stone Giants age.

### The Next Day (*kinda*)

We awake the next day to a full breakfast spread with pancakes, sausage, bacon, and *Oatmeal*. [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) has a mini-orgasm as he fill two plates of oatmeal.

[Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) goes off looking for *Korval*. [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) keeps calling *Korval* great and *Korval* doesn’t seem to like that. [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) expresses his desire to improve his farm business by gathering more potential plants. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) is wandering around looking for *Korval* and find [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) and him chatting. *Korval* expresses a need for caution as certain plants look the same but they can be highly deadly. He asks what is available at the [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) family farm to which he responds that they grow oats. *Korval* says he needs to do research on which is the best to bring but his initial gut reaction is flowers. He says to look for;

* A blue flower with 3 spikes on the back
* A flower with a jug like bottom
* A beautiful pink flower

Additionally, he recommends a soil from the farm dimension to improve the existing oats. [Noah the Boa](..\NPCs\Noah%20the%20Boa.md) shows up with some guest pass tuning forks for us to use to travel around the library.

[Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md) wants to read up on spells to clear his mind. He finishes his food and gets up to go deeper into the library. On his way, he stops by a particular giant that is staring at him.

[Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) and [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) went to the Arborear Plane. [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) found his flowers. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) communed with [Mielikki](..\NPCs\Gods\Mielikki.md) who actually showed up and gave information to [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md).
