# Donald Glover walks in and everything is on Fire

**DAYS TIL VOTE** - *19*

## Day 2

We open up to a seaweed area witnessing a tentacle monster attacking a durger. We engage in combat in an attempt to save and talk to the durger. The Durger is thrown at the ship window and he escapes to his sinking ship. The tentacle monster escapes and we begin speaking with the Durger who is named *Qwnt*.  *Qwnt* explains that the tentacle monster is a baby Kraken. We brought *Qwnt* on board cuz [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) sunk his ship. *Qwnt* urges us to hunt the Krakens to kill the mother. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) pushes back saying we have a timeline to keep. The Party agrees to keep to the timeline. Our directions align for now so *Qwnt* will come along but if our paths diverge, he either comes with us or will be stranded at sea.
