## Pre-session notes

* Details about the necklace that the [Chud Magician](..\NPCs\Chud%20Magician.md) wears. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) hunts for an accurate depiction of the necklace for the [Chud Magician](..\NPCs\Chud%20Magician.md). Quackily wants to go to the market to create a replica because it has some significance.
* [Lanniver](..\NPCs\Sir%20Lanniver%20Strayl.md) severed his connection to Ra when fighting Orcus. If he dies now, he is gone. He was one of the main leader when facing the Drow. He has killed many Drow
* [Zszrak Freiryn](..\NPCs\Zszrak%20Freiryn.md) is anxious about new proclamations are at rest for 137 days before the Drow Council votes and ratifies it into the Drow Constitution. Once it's added, it's next to impossible to remove short of felling the Drow Civilization.
* The series of roads between the UMD and the dock are off-limits. This happened a month prior to our arrival.
* The Great Boob is off limits and anyone that tries to approach will be detained

## Session Notes

[Zszrak](..\NPCs\Zszrak%20Freiryn.md) asks us "Who are you really?" in a scared/nervous tone. Kadulus seems to think he isn't going to report us. Gildo says that we are here to investigate the oddities going on. [Zszrak](..\NPCs\Zszrak%20Freiryn.md) sympathizes that other people don't know they are being mind controlled. [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) tells [Zszrak](..\NPCs\Zszrak%20Freiryn.md) that we are the Deadly Knightshades. [Zszrak](..\NPCs\Zszrak%20Freiryn.md) saves himself from fainting and asks who sent them and [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) mentions that [Vizian](..\NPCs\Vizian%20Daveer.md) sent us to help Drow kind. He called us weak because we don't have any power.

We have 2 weeks to stop the vote. We debate on attempting to convince the high families to vote against the Proclamation. [Zszrak](..\NPCs\Zszrak%20Freiryn.md) says that we should try to find out and stop the brainwashing. Something might be hiding at The Boob. The magical invisible tentacle doesn't like to be perceived and seems to avoid [Zszrak](..\NPCs\Zszrak%20Freiryn.md).

We decide to grab steal some onions and eat them in an attempt to stave off the [Demogorgon](..\NPCs\Demon%20Lords\Demogorgon.md) influence. Afterwards, we begin to make our way towards the docks/The Boob. Once there, it's pretty empty. Arriving at The Boob, it is heavily guarded and the windows have been blocked off to see inside the building. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) decides to recon inside The Boob while [Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md) messages [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) to relay information back to everyone. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) reports back there is living creature in the building from UMD. There are guards in the building as well. [Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md) tells [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) to go deeper in to get a better look. The creature is an aquatic creature. It might be a synthetic instead of flesh. It reminds [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) about the Deep Mother being sacrificed. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) says that is isn't being sacrificed but being studied. We discuss possibilites of what to do. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) climbs higher and recognizes that it's a creature that is in pain meant to habitat people. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) attempts to climb onto the creature to communicate with it. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) gets a closer look at the creature and the insides of it. He attempts to show the creature he is friendly but ends up scaring it a little. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) throws his voice to the other corner to provide cover and then dives and glides into safety. [Shaw](..\PCs\Shaw.md) investigates the magical power of the [Demogorgon](..\NPCs\Demon%20Lords\Demogorgon.md). The party start the return back to the inn.
We rest for the night and set out for Clawrift first thing in the morning. [Shaw](..\PCs\Shaw.md) and [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) start to traverse down the wall of the Rift in an attempt to find the depth of it. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) summons *Waukeen Phoenix* to help expand his range of vision. [Shaw](..\PCs\Shaw.md) knocks to reveal a secret passage and [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) signals for the rest of us to come down the wall. One by one everyone makes it down safely until [Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md) who beefs it and has to Misty Step to save himself.
