# Vizian Daveer

## Backstory

* He was a former demon researcher for the Drow / Menzoberanzan government
* He was kicked out after he refused to do cerdian experiments for fear of, oh say, releasing the demon lords onto the Underdark in fear of creating a black hole
* He was replaced by Dimar Ilalorgh, who continued his work and eventually released the demon lords into the Underdark
* Has history with [Chud Magician](Chud%20Magician.md)
