# Gracklestoog

* Across the [Darklake](Darklake.md) from [Sloobledop](Sloobledop.md)
* Biggest city in the Underdark
* Central trade hub of the Underdark
* They have LOTS of weapons there, can get any weapon you can think of at the "Blade Bazar"
* [Bupido](..\NPCs\Bupido.md) is very eager to go there for unclear reasons
* [JimJar](..\NPCs\JimJar.md) is interested in stopping there insofar as it's a segway to going to [Blingdenstone](Blingdenstone.md)
* [Sarith](..\NPCs\Sarith.md) is ok with stopping there insofar as it's a segway to [Neverlight Grove](Neverlight%20Grove.md)
* [Edith](..\NPCs\Edith.md) / [Dendril](..\NPCs\Prince%20Dendril.md) don't care, just want to get out of the Underdark
