# The Webbed Path and Tomb Raiding

The party continued down the webbed path. Using the dead Spectator, we spotted 2 quaggoths and 3 drow ahead of us, one of which was riding a spider. After only 2 seconds of debating how to approach them, [Tholraak](..\PCs\Tholraak.md) took some grease and dive bombed the group. One drow fell to his death but the rest got angry. Through some pew pews and bonks we managed to kill the lesser monkeys, but the leader lady on a spider jumped to the ceiling and cast a gigantic cone of fire with her wand with a red dragon head on the top of it, causing the entire party to fall the the bottom of the pit on some soft moss.  
The two gremlins that were escorting us fucked off before asking us to pay them (kek), [Globagool](..\NPCs\Globagool.md) said he was getting bad vibes from this place, and [Stool](..\NPCs\Stool.md) said we were near his home, [Neverlight Grove](..\Locations\Neverlight%20Grove.md). Close enough that he could tell us how to get there from where we were. We began walking through the caverns. On the way, [Tholraak](..\PCs\Tholraak.md) continued to help *Luna* with her shapeshifting while [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) and [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) failed to reach their gods. As we walked through the caverns, we found some red spicy moss that we recognized as what is used to make *fire wine*. One night we also found some lightning bugs that were really warm. We put them in a leather pouch with some moss for them to eat. One night, [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) heard a voice calling for help from a cavern. Walking into the cavern we discovered a room with a giant 1 in the middle of it with some paintings and a staircase going down. Going down the stairs to a bigger room, then a smaller room with 4 sarcophagi. Once we fucked with em 4 specters came out. We said their mom is fat until they were defeated.  
We also realized during this battle that using any magic in this crypt resulted in strange unintended effects such as *magic missiles*, *grease*, and [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) automatically having 1 failed saving throw for eternity. We totally didn't defile the sarcophagi and find some shinies. [Tholraak](..\PCs\Tholraak.md) moved one out of the way to reveal a trap door. [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md)'s leap of faith resulted in some crunchy bones but he found another sarcophagus with a *golden blanket* on it. Detecting no evil from it he put the golden blanket over his shoulder. The rest of the party climbed down via rope. [Tholraak](..\PCs\Tholraak.md) found an invisible box we eventually opened and found the goodies listed below in it. What was in the sarcophagus?
