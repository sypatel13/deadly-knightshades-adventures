# Prison Break; Part 1

[Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md) woke up being thrown into a cell in nothing but his undies. He was sent to dish duty and met a small man named [Bupido](..\NPCs\Bupido.md) claiming to be a god. He also decided to kick the chair of *Ilvara*, earning him 2 or 3 beatings. He was woken up to a thicc dummy aka [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) being thrown on him. They didn’t have long to talk before [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) was put on barrel duty, filling barrels with water. He met an elf named [Prince Dendril](..\NPCs\Prince%20Dendril.md) who claimed to be polymorphed into a quaggoth by a witch. That night [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) and [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) were thrown into the cell then put on lift duty. They went with a dwarf lady named [Edith](..\NPCs\Edith.md) and deep gnome named *Jim Jar* to lower barrels beneath the spider webs. They talked for a bit then a drow commander named *Jorlan* hinted that he was interested in making *Ilvara* look bad for replacing him as a lover with another drow named *Shore*.  
Later at lunch a mushroom was pushed by an orc for its food. [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) and [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) stepped in, but then *Ilvara* broke it up with [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md)'s tentacle wand, feeding orc to spiders. [Tholraak](..\PCs\Tholraak.md) joined the group in the cell, was assigned toilet cleaning duty, where he met a fish man named [Shushar The Awoken](..\NPCs\Shushar%20The%20Awoken.md) and a manic drow named *Sareth*. In the middle of planning how they would escape, demons attacked the prison. *Jorlan* used this excuse to open the cell doors. The party fought some drow in the armory and picked up some weapons/armor. They went to the royal quarters and found some of their stuff and then some in fancy chests.
