# Intro to [Gauntlgrym](..\Locations\Gauntlgrym.md)

The party began following the fire trail left by the fox on the way to [Gauntlgrym](..\Locations\Gauntlgrym.md). We came to a point where the path split and their map said to go one way but the fire trail went another. We camped for the night but in the morning decided to stick to the fire trail. Nothing bad happened. Eventually we came across a huge stone door and the fire trail disappeared behind us as we approached. We knocked on the door, to which a little window in the door moved and a dwarf said "Oh my god they're here!" and the giant gate/door opened. We were greeted by a lavish looking dwarf who said to call him *Koolick Heavyjaw*. He addressed us each as Master, even pronouncing [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md)'s real name perfectly, although he did give [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) a funny look any time he spoke. He informed us that [Edith](..\NPCs\Edith.md) had arrived beforehand and informed them of our adventures. He then gestured to a set of 3 minecarts and gestured to get in. We were very confused but he explained they were magic and we got in, [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) got a cart to himself as the caboose. We went through a tunnel and first came to a giant opening that seemed like the Residential District. There were a lot of houses but we noticed that there were some areas that weren't very populated. *Koolick* explained that they had relatively recently gotten the city back from the drow and were still rebuilding. Next we went through another tunnel and came to an area that was smaller than the previous one but was still quite large, the Holy District aka Tabernacle. In the center of it was a mountain with a spiral path going up. Along the path we could see altars devoted to different gods. At the top [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) noticed a man who was sitting in perfect meditation posture basking in what looked like direct sunlight. In this light, [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) also saw the outline of a hooded woman.  
Just as they noticed this, we went into another tunnel. At our final stop we arrived at some kind of palace. There was a crowd outside of our fans waiting for us, cheering as we came to a stop. [Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md) had big titty goth gfs, guys in dragon costume (scalies lmao) for [Tholraak](..\PCs\Tholraak.md), Kenku for [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md), meatheads for [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md), and "Make [Gauntlgrym](..\Locations\Gauntlgrym.md) great again" dudes for [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) (aka the AltLight). The mine cart continued into the palace until we came across a Throne Room, but it was big and empty with just a throne in the middle. We investigated it and [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) sat in it, being launched far away. Cackling broke out as another dwarf entered the room. He introduced himself as the [King Bartemus](..\NPCs\King%20Bartemus.md) and said "they always sit in the chair". [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) made a joke about him being the king of chair pranks but the king found this funny. The king showed us a circle of chairs and asked us to sit. There wasn't one for [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) but [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) let him have his since he didn't trust their chairs anymore. The king said [Edith](..\NPCs\Edith.md) had come ahead and told him the stories of our group. We confirmed his stories using [Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md)' skeletons as actors and [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) as a narrator. After hearing our stories, the king told *Koolick* to bring us to a house and let us get situated. He said the threat of demons was being felt everywhere and there would be a big meeting between superiors in [Gauntlgrym](..\Locations\Gauntlgrym.md) in a few days. Two names of importance he brought up were *Mageria* of the Hapers and *Zelron Roaringhorn*.  
*Koolick* brought us to our [Gauntlgrym](..\Locations\Gauntlgrym.md) House. We appreciated staying somewhere besides a tavern or prison cell for the first time in months. He showed us around but we were most excited when we reached the kitchen and found *Cornhole*. [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) took a bath for the first time in forever, even finding a few pieces of gold in his dried blood and grease folds. *Luna* did the same.  
The first thing we did was take a minecart to the holy mountain. At the base we came across a very chill dood in front of a statue of a hooded woman with **\[something\]** in her right hand and an empty outstretched left hand. There was an altar with food on it but something smelt rank. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) approached him and started asking questions. He introduced himself as Shaggy and started explaining the religion he was devoted to. He explained that the altar was a food bank where people could take or leave what they could/wanted but apologized for the smell as someone had dropped off a giant organ and then a bleeding heart that he was still cleaning up.  
He worshiped a goddess named [Mielikki](..\NPCs\Gods\Mielikki.md) who is attuned to preserving nature and animals. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md)'s eyes lit up at the sound of this. Seeing his interest, Shaggy asked if he wanted to be initiated. He held out a mushroom(?) for [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) to take and told him to, like, just go under where the statue's hand was dudeee. He then placed a bow in the statue's hand. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) obliged and had the weirdest trip. He was teleported to a forest where he saw an orc caught in a bear trap. He approached the orc who refused to communicate with him but seemed very aggressive. After some time of gaining the orc's trust, he was able to get the bear trap off, to which the orc took off running away. Suddenly a woman approached him and commended him for his choice of peace/preservation. She walked with him to the top of a hill and introduced herself as [Mielikki](..\NPCs\Gods\Mielikki.md). She took [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md)'s bow and planted it in the ground. For months, [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) walked around with [Mielikki](..\NPCs\Gods\Mielikki.md) and learned the ways of the forest. After a while they came back to find his bow had grown into a tree. She had [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) chop it down and make his bow again, but now it had a leaf growing out of it, as if it was *living*.  
[Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) returned to the land of the sober. From the rest of the party's perspective, it looked like he was just standing still and bugging out, doing the same motions but extremely fast. Shaggy told him that he did a good job and that [Mielikki](..\NPCs\Gods\Mielikki.md) must like him, doood. He also said [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) doesn't need to waste a bonus action on Hunter's Mark anymore.{{did this also make his bow magical??. He also got a dope tee shirt with a print of the unicorn in it}} As we were leaving, [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) said that he liked *Shaggy* and the food bank, but the feeling wasn’t mutual when [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) mentioned he was a follower of [Waukeen](..\NPCs\Gods\Waukeen.md). Continuing up The Tabernacle, we arrived at the altar dedicated to [Waukeen](..\NPCs\Gods\Waukeen.md). There was a big circular mural of her face/symbol on the floor and a pompous looking man at the altar. Upon [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) mentioning that he was a Waulkeenar, the doosh questioned his dedication since he didn't bear her symbol or donate any money to the altar. After hearing that we had met [Waukeen](..\NPCs\Gods\Waukeen.md) and [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) smiting a random box, the doosh was taken aback that it meant [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) was an *Overgold* but he still needed to go through the ceremony. He told everyone to get the most putrid garbage they could find and bring it back to the altar tomorrow. Continuing up the walkway, we reached the top where the Guru was. He was sitting in the middle of a circle with 7 spokes going towards the middle. He introduced himself as [Guru Guidingcalling](..\NPCs\Guru%20Guidingcalling.md) and we ended the session here.
