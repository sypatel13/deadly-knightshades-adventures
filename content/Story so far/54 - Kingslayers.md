# Kingslayers

*Errd Blackskull* finished introducing us to her scientist who was an expert on slimes. He said he'd study [Globagool](..\NPCs\Globagool.md) while we fou-I mean met the king. We told him a little information and just said not to hurt him. *Blackskull* said we could just walk up to the castle and knock. We asked for any more info and she said the same as stone giants, that he has been drinking a lot lately but can still lead overall, so nothing has been done. She also said his favorite drink was fire wine, which they make at the nearby brewery. We stopped by The Bazaar on the way to the brewery. We informed *Ms. Henstack* on how the darrow were getting their money and she gave us a map of the underdark in return, specifically pointing out Blindenstone. At the brewery, the duragur who answered the door said they only serve duragur, but [Tholraak](..\PCs\Tholraak.md) convinced him with moneys. We carried the cask through the city to the king.  
Arriving at the king's keep, we kept yelling "Hello" but no one answered. We just walked up to the door and opened it. Harmoniously singing Hello, we came upon *The King* and his consort standing at the end of the throne room. After explaining we weren't his jesters, we showed him the cask of fire wine. He asked us to bring it forward, lowering the invisible wall in front of us. [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md)'s ass burned the closer he got to them. After not ignoring the burning, combat was started with "bonk?" The consort did beeg damidge to [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) that he could not recover from. Through slashing, spells, and arrows we managed to kill the succubus, causing the king to come to his senses.  
Once the *The King* was sane, he said alcohol was stoopid. He saw [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) and gave us his *metal arm* as a reward for saving him. We asked him about the factions and he said that without *Thumberchad* the city would have no way of using their forges and it would be a disaster. [Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md) also brought the succubus back from the dead and we asked her some questions. She explained [Graz'zt](..\NPCs\Demon%20Lords\Graz'zt.md) is coming and having this city under control would please him. She also said she works for [Malcanthet](..\NPCs\Malcanthet.md), the queen succubus. We went back to *Errd* and her blob specialist. He asked us to describe the temple we found [Globagool](..\NPCs\Globagool.md) was in and it sounded like a temple to a demon of ooze named [Juiblex](..\NPCs\Demon%20Lords\Juiblex.md) aka **Faceless Lord** aka **Oozing Hunger**.
