# Orcus Cult; Part 3, Slaying Trees

After the party took a short rest from the horrors they had witnessed in the dungeon so far, we explored the room we were in. We found notes talking about experimentation and mentioning "\[4 somethings become one\]". We also found a wand made of a spine with a skull atop it as described in the notes on [Orcus](..\NPCs\Demon%20Lords\Orcus.md), but a little different/lesser as it wasn't in pristine condition and seemed like a remake. We decided to give it to [Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md) as it felt good in his hands but stinky/weird to everyone else. Continuing through the double doors, we found a hallway, which [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) found another secret hallway in.  
We decided to split the party with [Tholraak](..\PCs\Tholraak.md), *Luna*, [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md), and some skeletons going one way and [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md), [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md), [Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md), and skeletons going another way. [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) casted *Haste* on [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) and we caught the tree fucks by surprise. There were four lesser wooden bois originally, but as the fight went on a big wooden Ent came out of the giant tree in the middle of the room. The rest of the party dealt with the small trees while [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) poked at the big one's toe with his sword and got knocked on his ass repeatedly by its gargantuan smacks. Throughout the battle, [Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md) felt the wand demand him to siphon life and deal as much damage as possible to the trees, putting his own life at risk in the process. [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) was a holy man and healed the party a ton in the middle of the fight, saving [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) and *Luna* from the brink of wooden death. He also increased [Tholraak](..\PCs\Tholraak.md)'s strength for shits and giggles. [Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md) sacrificed many skeletons for the good of the party. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) fired a million arrows, utilizing the *Haste*. Once the big tree had taken enough damidge, it's chest opened up to reveal a dragon's heart. We focused on it until it was destroyed.  
Afterwards, the party took a second to catch their breath. [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) sat upon the stump, [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) sacrificed the dragon heart to his god, [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) praised [Waukeen](..\NPCs\Gods\Waukeen.md) (which *Luna* approved of, suck it [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md)), and [Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md) gathered the bones of *Rancanzor*. On the way out, [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) thought to set the mural of [Orcus](..\NPCs\Demon%20Lords\Orcus.md) ablaze. Seeing this, [Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md)' new strange wand commanded him to shoot a blast of necrotic energy at [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) for max damage. [Tholraak](..\PCs\Tholraak.md) and [Lester](..\PCs\Lester%20Charlesworth.md) stood there shocked while [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) and [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) wrestled it out of his hands. Upon leaving the dungeon, we came across a small flame fox. [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) began to pet it, but then it opened its mouth and a voice said "Is this thing on? Adventurers, you are needed in [Gauntlgrym](..\Locations\Gauntlgrym.md), what do you call yourselves?" to which [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) replied ***Deadly Knightshade***™️, then it scampered off at the speed of light, leaving behind a trail of fire.
