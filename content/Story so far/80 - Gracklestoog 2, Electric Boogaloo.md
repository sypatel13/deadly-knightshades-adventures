# [Gracklestoog](..\Locations\Gracklestoog.md) 2, Electric Boogaloo

Lester crystal shenanigans on rooftop  
Used the spell that was in [Kalelgoni](..\NPCs\Kalelgoni.md). Turned out to be a Deck of Some Things™. The party members drew various cards, mostly for the better. [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) has a card granting him a Wish.  
Made our way to [Gracklestoog](..\Locations\Gracklestoog.md). We stealthed in using Quackity’s *thing*. We walked towards Blackskull’s office, noticed a dent in the ground of a giant quadruped. Continued to Blackskull’s office. We found her (implied) dead body, archery trophy, and a crystal ball. We gave her a viking burial at sea by sending her out on a raft while [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) shot a flamed arrow at the raft.  
Continuing on our way to the brewery, we walked through a gigantic tunnel that was free of debris but heard something big making noise. [Tholraak](..\PCs\Tholraak.md) took point and investigated as a spider. He came across a huge Balrog that was tending to its wounds. He asked if it wanted help, but it quickly turned hostile. It was clearly injured and we killed it quickly, but it burst into flames once it died. The lightning sword and fire whip that [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) and [Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md) (respectively) were ogling disappeared as it died.
The party finally got to the brewery, which was surprisingly intact. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) investigated the brewery to discover that the equipment was intact as well. However, a big demon appeared to be guarding it. Acknowledging that they were probably better off saving their strength for Malcanthet, Deadly Nightshade carried on towards the former King’s Castle.
