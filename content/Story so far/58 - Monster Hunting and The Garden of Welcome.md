# Monster Hunting and The Garden of Welcome

In the meantime, we decided to seek out the monsters plaguing the hunter circle members. We were able to seek out and kill the *Grick Alpha* fairly quickly, not before it grabbed [Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md) and tossed his salad. Next we sought out the *Shambling Mound*. It was difficult at first because the forest had a fuck ton of vines, but eventually we found it. It tried to disappear underground but our fearless leader [Tholraak](..\PCs\Tholraak.md) grabbed onto its tail and killed it with his bare hands. [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) utilized this time to practice his mounted combat on the mama bear, which started off shaky but she got more comfortable with some help from [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md).  
We returned to [Basidia](..\NPCs\Basidia.md) with the news that we had slain the monsters giving the Hunter's circle a hard time, to which she rewarded us with a potion of our choice from what she could make. She then told us to explore the Garden of Welcome. Although she could not go herself, she wanted us to gather intel and just said to not get killed. When we asked for a status on the medicine, she emphasized again that it would take a long time. We thanked her and made our way to the Garden, which was a huge plateau atop a staircase.  
We could hear general sadness and despair, but when we got to the top we saw a bunch of decomposing, still living, corpses crying out in pain. We recognized one of them as a drow that was stalking us in Grakelstoog. She gave us a spider medallion with her rotting hand and asked us to put her out of her misery. Seeing this as a fair trade after the party did an arcane check, divine sense, and detect magic, [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) obliged. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) found her stuff in a nearby pile, finding the following items:

* +2 leather armor (Quackity)
* +2 shortsword (Gildo)
* Bag of holding (Kadulus)
* Scroll of Remove Curse
* Scroll of Spider Climb

Walking a tad further, we discovered a gigantic mushroom ~100ft tall that looked good and healthy. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) said "quack", which [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) perfectly understood meant the undead corpses in the ground were feeding the tree.  
We heard an odd noise and out of the ground popped a large pupa with ooze coming out of it. It said telepathically and aloud "welcome to the wedding, are you with the bride or the groom? Doesn't matter, we shall see the great union." Out of the ground rose 2 drow and 2 michonids who began attacking us. We did away with its lackies very quickly, but it would heal by consuming the corpses during the fight. With a smite directly from [Waukeen](..\NPCs\Gods\Waukeen.md) herself, this thing was finally slain and sank into the ground.
