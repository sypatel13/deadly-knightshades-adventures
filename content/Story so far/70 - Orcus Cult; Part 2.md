# Orcus Cult; Part 2, Meeting Luna’s Mommy

The party continued down the [Orcus](..\NPCs\Demon%20Lords\Orcus.md) cult dungeon. We came to an emptyish room. [Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md) was able to find 2 hidden closets. One had some gold and (Guy Fieri hood) robes, the other had a body or something. The path ahead was blocked with vines, [Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md) was able to send a skeleton down it to discover an empty room with a big barricade blocking another hallway. While the party was trying to figure out a way to burn the vines, the skeleton ran back through them, ripping out the rock above and causing a collapse. After sifting through the rubble, the party continued onward. [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) looked through a little peephole in the barricade, only to barely miss an arrow shot through it. [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) began talking to the ones that sent the arrow. Long story short, we were able to handle the situation diplomatically. It turned out to be a bunch of Kobolds, weak and harmless dragonfolk. *Luna* wanted to destroy them but [Tholraak](..\PCs\Tholraak.md) said they were fine. We let them live since we could do away with them if we broke wind. They claimed that *Rancanzor* had sent them but they were holding their ground since they were so weak. [Tholraak](..\PCs\Tholraak.md) kept having outbursts like "wait your turn" and "he already said that" but was a cock about explaining who/what he was talking to/about. We carried on down the dungeon to find the roots of the tree from above with water flowing from them, covering a stairwell. We had to fight two more worm bois but they refused to touch the flowing water for some reason.  
Following [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md)'s "deeper down", we came across the skeletal remains of a dragon. The Kobolds immediately rushed in and bowed to it. Her eyes flashed red and communicated telepathically with the party: "What took you so long!?" It turned out to be *Rancanzor*, a red dragon who was killed thousands of years ago but her soul was trapped there via her heart by the cult. She demanded we set her soul free by killing it. Talking to her more, we realized that she was *Luna*'s mother. Knowing that *Rancanzor* had been dead for thousands of years, she was shocked and curious how old she actually was. There didn't seem to be any bond between them as the dead dragon claimed she had laid thousands of eggs in her lifetime. [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) and [Tholraak](..\PCs\Tholraak.md) remained with her until she felt ready to move on. The party adorned the robes we picked up earlier in the dungeon, acting like the cultists they once belonged to. The first room we came across had no life but looked like it was used for experimentation. We discovered a closet that had a handless body in it. A hand then jumped out at us, but [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md) neutralized it and threw it back in the closet.  
We continued into the following room where we saw three beings, 2 worm bois and a very scary *thing*. [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) took the opportunity to run in and scare them with his *Necrotic Shroud*. [Kadulus](..\PCs\Kadulus.md) hurled a meteor which vaporized a worm boi and badly hurt the other baddies. The scary thing hurt to look at and even knocked down [Quackity](..\PCs\Quackity.md). [Gildo](..\PCs\Gildo%20Stormborn.md) managed to slash the last two to death with his necrotic damidge, but could sense a greater evil through a set of double doors…
